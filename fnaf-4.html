About

FNaF 4 Online is a fan-made browser adaptation of the original Five Nights at Freddy's 4 by Scott Cawthon. 🎮 This free-to-play version allows players to experience the terrifying gameplay directly from their web browsers—no download required!

Dive into the horror from the comfort of your browser and survive the night against nightmarish animatronics in a child's bedroom setting. Ideal for those seeking a quick fright without installing the full game.

Description

🕹️ In FNaF 4 Online, your objective remains the same: survive from midnight to 6 A.M. against nightmare versions of Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, Foxy, and Fredbear. However, instead of a downloadable game, you play entirely in your browser.

  • Web-Based: No installation needed. Play instantly on Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
  • Mobile Compatible: Many fan versions are optimized for touch-based controls.
  • Fast Loading: Lightweight HTML5 versions provide smooth performance.

👾 This online version replicates the chilling experience of the original game with simplified mechanics. Use your mouse or keyboard to check doors, shine your flashlight, and listen carefully for approaching enemies.

🎯 Challenge yourself through multiple nights of increasing difficulty. Some online versions even include bonus nights or fan-made content.

⚠️ Basic Controls:
- Move between doors: Mouse left/right or arrow keys
- Flashlight: Click or press Ctrl
- Close doors: Click and hold or press Space

🧠 Tips: Always listen carefully. Animatronics often signal their approach with subtle sounds. Quick reflexes and sound judgment are key to survival!
